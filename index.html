About Me

Recent Software Engineering Graduate

I am a recent software engineering graduate from Ontario Tech University, earning a Bachelor's in Software Engineering (Honours) with Highest Distinction. I finished with a 3.94 cGPA (4.3 Scale) and won multiple competitions including 1st place Capstone Project in the Faculty of Engineering Electrical, Computer, and Software Engineering out of 32 projects. I won 1st place in NASA Space Apps Hackathon 2024 and was nominated as the Global Nominee representing Ontario Tech on the global scale, which led to a CSA internship offer. Most recently, I won 3rd place at Ontario Engineering Competition in 2025, representing Ontario Tech in the Programming category.

Software Engineer at Atsign

I am a Software Engineer at Atsign, taking the lead on projects in C/C++, Embedded Systems, IoT, DevOps, Automated Testing, and App Development. I started as an intern in June 2022, was brought on as a core engineer, and now work as a full-time engineer. I've led projects including the C SDK, C++ SDK, and contributed to our flagship product NoPorts in the C Daemon section and NoPorts Desktop (Flutter app). As one of the lead intern coordinators and talent acquisitioners, I lead recruitment and intern mentorship. I've represented Atsign at conferences including Sensors Converge 2023, CES 2024 (Consumer Electronics Show), and SecTor 2024.

3D Printing Enthusiast & Robotics Mentor

I mentor FRC team 5596 in Scarborough, Ontario, focusing on robotics, computer vision, and Java programming. I've served as a judge advisor for multiple FIRST Lego League competitions and continue to support young engineers in their robotics journey. As a 3D printing enthusiast, I own a Bambu Lab X1C with 2 AMS units, exploring the intersection of rapid prototyping and robotics development.
