## Experience

This was another year of volunteering as the Judge Advisor at my high school's annual FIRST Lego League robotics tournament.

I took a break of Judge Advising in 2024 to focus on capstone, but since I graduated in June 2025, I had time to judge advise this year around.

Judge Advising was the same as any year: recruit judge volunteers (this year we had about 20 of them, if i recall correctly), ensure they are signed up, sign the correct waivers, and are trained for the big day.
